About Us

Your favorite dishes – one tap away!

What is NjamNjam?

NjamNjam is a modern, student-created website designed for fast and fun food ordering. From mouth-watering burgers to sweet desserts, we let you explore a full menu and place your order in just a few clicks.

Why did we build it?

We wanted a simple and stylish way to browse dishes, choose your favorites, and see your cart update live — all using modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

How does it work?

Our interactive menu lets you add meals directly to your cart. All selections are saved locally using your browser’s storage. You can return anytime and pick up where you left off!
